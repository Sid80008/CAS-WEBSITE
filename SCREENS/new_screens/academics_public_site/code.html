School Rules & Regulations

Strict adherence to these regulations ensures a safe, respectful, and highly productive academic environment for all students.

1

Punctuality & Arrival

Students must report to the campus by 7:50 AM. Latecomers will be marked absent for the first period and require a specialized gate pass from the discipline committee.

2

Uniform & Grooming Standards

Complete, neat, and school-approved uniform is compulsory. Identity cards must be worn at all times. Improper grooming will result in disciplinary action.

3

Campus Behavior & Respect

Respectful conduct towards peers, faculty, and support staff is non-negotiable. Bullying, vandalism, or the use of foul language leads to immediate suspension.

4

Prohibition of Electronics

Mobile phones, smartwatches, and all unauthorized electronic devices are strictly prohibited on campus grounds. Confiscated items will only be returned to parents.
