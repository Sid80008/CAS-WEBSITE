Admission Process

A simple, transparent, and guided journey to join the Central Academy antah community.

Step 1: Registration

Begin by submitting the online inquiry form. Our admissions team will reach out to schedule a campus tour and provide the detailed application packet.

Required Documents

  • check_circle Birth Certificate (Original + Photocopy)
  • check_circle Previous School Records/TC
  • check_circle Recent Passport Photographs
1
2

Step 2: Assessment

Prospective students participate in a grade-appropriate interactive assessment. This helps us understand their academic standing and learning style to ensure the best fit.

Grades 1-5: Interactive Session Grades 6-12: Written Evaluation

Step 3: Enrollment

Upon successful assessment, an official offer of admission is extended. Complete the enrollment by submitting the final paperwork and securing your seat with the initial fee deposit.
