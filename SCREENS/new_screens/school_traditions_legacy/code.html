Our Institutional Traditions

For three decades, our daily rituals and annual events have shaped the character, discipline, and shared spirit of our student body. These traditions are the heartbeat of Central Academy Anta.

The Pillars of Our Culture

wb_sunny

Morning Assembly

The cornerstone of our daily routine. Every morning begins with a collective gathering that instills discipline, unity, and a moment of reflection before the academic day commences.

flag

National Days

Celebrating our nation's heritage with grandeur. Republic Day and Independence Day are marked by flag hoisting, parades, and cultural performances that foster deep patriotism.

emoji_events

Annual Sports Day

A highly anticipated tradition fostering healthy competition and physical excellence. Track and field events bring out the true spirit of sportsmanship among all houses.

festival

Cultural Exhibitions

Showcasing artistic and academic brilliance. Our annual exhibitions allow students to present projects, art, and performances, celebrating creativity across the bilingual curriculum.
