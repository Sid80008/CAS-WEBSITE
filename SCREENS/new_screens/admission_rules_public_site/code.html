Fee Payment Rules

  • payments

    Quarterly Payments

    School fees must be paid quarterly by the 10th of the first month of each quarter (April, July, October, January).

  • warning

    Late Fees

    A late fee of ₹50 per day will be charged after the due date till the end of the month.

  • credit_card

    Payment Methods

    Fees can be paid online through the school portal, via Cheque/Demand Draft in favor of "Central Academy antah", or via POS terminal at the fee counter.

  • cancel

    Name Struck Off

    If fees are not paid by the end of the first month of the quarter, the student's name may be struck off the rolls.
