Library Guidelines

Maintaining a harmonious environment for all scholars.

1

Silence is Mandatory

Strict silence must be maintained in and around the library premises to ensure an undisturbed study environment.

2

Card Usage

Books are issued solely against the student's personal Library Card. Cards are non-transferable.

3

Return Policy

Issued books must be returned within 14 days. Late returns will incur a nominal fine as per school policy.

4

Care of Resources

Marking, underlining, or defacing books in any manner is strictly prohibited. Damaged books must be replaced by the borrower.
